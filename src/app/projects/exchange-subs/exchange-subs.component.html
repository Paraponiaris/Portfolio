Exchange Subs a Full-Stack API UI Application

Exchange Subs is designed for social media artists who want to grow their audience by engaging in follow-for-follow exchanges with other artists. This behavior is already prevalent on social platforms, but our application provides a structured way to monetize and marketize this organic interaction.

Here's how it works: Users create a profile within the app and add the URLs of the social media platforms where they wish to gain followers. When a user follows another artist on a specified platform, they send a "ticket" through the app. This ticket essentially says, "I've followed you on [platform name]; please follow me back."

The application also includes a rating system. This allows users to provide feedback on their follow-for-follow experiences, helping to ensure accountability and optimize the effectiveness of the mechanism for all participants.
